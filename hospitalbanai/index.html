About Us

Our Vision

  • A high quality, technologically advanced, patient centric healthcare system in Bangladesh.
  • Adequate sophisticated medical & surgical treatment facilities for our people which are now inadequate (Medical: ICU/CCU/NICU/PICU, Dialysis, Chemotherapy. Surgical: Cardiothoracic surgery, Neurosurgery, Plastic/reconstructive surgery, Bariatric surgery, Head/neck surgery, Assisted Reproductive surgery, Urology).
  • Regional level availability of above mentioned sophisticated, clinical services, so that people dependency on Dhaka reduced.
  • Better, efficient, professional hospital/healthcare sector management, operation, sales/marketing in country.
  • Bangladesh become self-reliant on medical equipment, devices, kits, reagents, medical furniture now import.
  • International standard super specialty, multi-specialty hospital establishment in regional & national level.
  • International standard Medical College, University, Nursing College/university establishment in Bangladesh.

Healthcare management:

  • Taking partial or full management, operational, marketing, business responsibility of hospital/healthcare project, if required

  • We have more than 100 different types of project design already prepared, ranging from 20 lac Bangladeshi taka to over 2 billion US Dollar (over 20000 crore BDT).
  • We can adjust, plan & design projects with any budget amount.
  • If we cannot be like family members, trusted & respected by our clients, we feel a bit discomfort for doing works for that client.
  • We believe client & hospital/healthcare project consultant formal terms should not be like CEO/Chairman vs hired Consultant. This creates gaps & harms the project.
  • We do not believe in marketing using Facebook, LinkedIn posts, YouTube videos, or other digital marketing methods. We hope that our clients would be sure of our honesty, integrity, professionalism, and technical ability before they call us.
  • We greatly focus on our reputation & respect in the market much more than work or consultancy fees.
  • We do not do all works for money; if it seems to us that doing any particular project might harm our integrity, we would not do it.
  • Our shareholders & partners are sound professionals with notable positions in our own fields, where we are engaged.
  • This company might be the first (or among very few) of its kind in hospital/healthcare Advisory/Consultancy services in Bangladesh.
  • For progressing the healthcare sector/systems, this company was long overdue. We would try to help our domestic clients, our foreign partners/companies, and the Government through our best efforts to increase the quality, sophistication, and advancement of our healthcare sector.
  • We do not follow history or tradition much; we follow our heart, our desire to try to change our healthcare system and its future.

  • We focus on client’s mission, vision, expectations from us and prepare project design, technical/operational details of projects according to client’s expectations.
  • We do not suggest our clients for big budget, risky project (if client don’t have huge budget), we do project design which can ensure best possible Return On Investment (ROI) using the client budget/fund.
  • We support client for realizing funds (if required) through all of our technical, operational, networks.
  • We work for clients dream realization, as his/her family member.
  • We firmly stand with honesty, integrity, commitment, reputation. If a client project vision is not commercially viable project, we just explain that to him/her and say we do not require consultancy fees. Please do not do this project.
  • We only engage with highly reputed renowned Architect, Civil Engineer, MEP/HVAC, Interior Decoration consultants for project infrastructure design & construction.
  • We only engage with renowned international brands/companies or leading & renowned domestic medical equipment, devices and related products supplier.
  • We take full responsibility not only for making hospital/healthcare project but also all types of training, coaching, technical/operational/managerial support/guidance for that project if required.
